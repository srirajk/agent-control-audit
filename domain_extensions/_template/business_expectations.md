# Business Expectations Template

## Business Process

Describe the workflow the agent supports.

## Decisions And Authority

List what the agent can:

- Answer.
- Recommend.
- Draft.
- Route.
- Execute.

List what requires human approval.

## Policies And SOPs

List relevant policies, procedures, playbooks, escalation rules, and exception handling rules.

## Required Output Behavior

Describe required disclaimers, explanations, citations, reason codes, or handoff language.

## Failure And Escalation

Describe when the agent must stop, escalate, ask for more information, or use a safe fallback.
