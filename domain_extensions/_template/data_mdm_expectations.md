# Data And MDM Expectations Template

## Source Systems

List source-of-truth systems and approved retrieval/data sources.

## Entity Definitions

Define domain entities and relationships.

Examples:

- Customer.
- Account.
- Counterparty.
- Merchant.
- Case.
- Alert.
- Transaction.
- Policy.
- Document.

## MDM Rules

Describe entity resolution, deduplication, hierarchy, relationship, ownership, householding, and survivorship rules.

## Data Quality Rules

Describe freshness, completeness, lineage, reconciliation, schema validation, and missing-data escalation.

## Data Classification

Describe public, internal, confidential, PII, PHI, PCI, MNPI, regulated record, and secret handling.

## Retrieval Boundaries

Describe approved corpora, tenant filters, business-line filters, jurisdiction filters, source freshness, and citation expectations.

## Safe Fixtures

Describe synthetic or redacted data that may be used in evals.
